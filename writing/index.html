FICTION WRITING

* i first learned i loved to write fiction when i was in fifth grade.

* before that, i loved making comics about fictional superheroes. then when my fifth grade teacher gave me one of the blank journals she had leftover at the end of the school year, i began to love writing my own chapter books as well.

* this love for writing only intensified with the discovery of roleplaying communities and fanfiction on the internet. eventually, my love for writing became so prominent that i earned my first writing-based award: best excellence in english language arts, an award i received after completing middle school.

* i proceeded to win more awards both in school and on the internet, which further encouraged my passion for writing!

* now, i'm currently working on drafting an original novel, and i plan on revising it and querying it to agents. i hope that sharing my process here as well as tips that have helped me can help others!

* i'll probably also write writing advice articles on my blog, so keep an eye out for that

* if anyone is looking for specific advice, feel free to email me and i may assist!
